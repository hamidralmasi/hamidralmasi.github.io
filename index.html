I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC). I work in BITS Networked Systems Lab under the supervision of Prof. Balajee Vamanan. I am broadly interested in networked systems with a focus on data center applications and programmable data planes. Recently, I have been focusing on creating efficient distributed machine learning systems. Prior to UIC, I got my M.Sc. from Sharif University of Technology and B.Sc. from University of Tehran.
